Clinic Location


The Toronto Allergists Clinic is located centrally and is accessible by TTC.

Closest subway: St Patrick Station

Physicians wishing to refer to Dr. Jason K Lee can send a referral letter with their ministry of health MOH numbers to Dr. Jason Lee's toll free fax number.

Canadian patients with OHIP or Interim Federal Health Plan coverage must be referred by a medical doctor.

Non OHIP patients or patients from outside of Canada are advised to contact our office for further direction.
